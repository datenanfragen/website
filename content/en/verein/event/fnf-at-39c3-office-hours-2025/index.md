Have you encountered problems with a data subject request? You think think someone is processing your data illegally and you want help filing a report? Or you want to volunteer with datarequests.org and want some help getting started? Meet up with us to talk data protection and how to make use of the GDPR.

Note: Due to German law, when it comes to specific advice, we need to refer you to a lawyer. But we can give you general advice and exchange experiences.
